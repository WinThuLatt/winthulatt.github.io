Did I tell you that I was using LibGDX to render really cool animations after that detecting the features? Sure, OpenCV coordinates and LibGDx screen coordinates are , in one word, different. I had a conversion function to do just that for me. Sadly I do not have the source code anymore and it was over a year since I implemented it. I still have two short videos that I recorded.
